How I Built This Blog with HTML + Netlify

Posted: May 13, 2025

I wanted a soft, minimal space to write — so I built this blog using basic HTML, CSS, and Netlify. No CMS. No clutter. Just focused creativity.

Why I Chose This Approach

  • It’s fast to build and easy to update
  • I get full creative control
  • I’m learning real dev skills while writing

Later, I may evolve this into an Astro or React-powered blog — but for now, this quiet launch feels just right.
